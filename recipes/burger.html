Burger

The hamburger or just commonly referred to as just a burger, is an iconic and classic take on American cusine. The dish most commonly served with beef as it's primary choice of protein but the very concept of a burger can use any type of protein like chicken or turkey.

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • 1 pound of ground protein of your choice
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Shredded onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Any sauces you like

Steps:

  1. Grab your protein of choice and put in a large bowl.
  2. Crack two eggs and shred one onion in the bowl of protein (you can use whatever spices you'd like).
  3. Mix the Ingredients around thoroughly and form into a patty.
  4. Pan fry the patty on each side.
  5. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun and top with Lettuce, Tomato, and any sauces you'd like.
  6. Enjoy!
