Pasta

Pasta is one of the most famous dishes in the world and is the pinnacle of Italian cusine with many different variations to choose from The complete concept of the dish includes some type of pasta shape or variation along with a rich sauce that coats and accents the pasta.

Ingredients:

  • A bag of any pasta shape
  • One can of Tomato puree
  • One Shredded onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Any spices you'd like
  • Your favorite cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat a pot of water until slightly bubbling.
  2. Check the pre-determined amount of time it takes to cook the pasta on the bag it's packaged in.
  3. Put the pasta in the boiling water and set a timer.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, grab a pan and lightly oil with Olive Oil.
  5. Throw in two cloves of minced garlic and one shredded onion and cook them until the garlic is lightly browned and the onions turn soft.
  6. Once the garlic is lightly browned and the onions turn soft, add one can of Tomato puree and give it a good mix.
  7. Add in your pasta once it's done cooking and add a small amount of pasta water to incorporate the pasta and the sauce together.
  8. Enjoy!
